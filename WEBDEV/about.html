FAQ

Frequently-Asked Questions

How do I request a website?

Go to the Contact page and fill out the necessary information. Press submit, and you will be emailed shortly on the status of your website!

When do I have to pay?

You won't have to pay until you are sent a preview of the website. Then, if you like it, you will be sent billing information along with the completed website. If you don't want it, you won't be charged.

What should I include in my request?

Include your name (optional), email (for contact purposes), the type of your website, and a detailed description of your website. Include links to images, layout diagrams, text to use in your website, and any other resources you would like to provide. If you are unsure about your design choices, give a vague description and we will create it for you.

What are Promo Codes and how do I get one?

Promo Codes can give you excellent deals on your website fees, such as one page without charge. You can get a Promo Code by signing up to recieve offers when you submit your request.

What do I do once I recieve my website?

Your website will be sent to you as an HTML file. You can preview it by dragging the file into your browser, and once you have ownership of a domain, you can use the file for your website through hosting services.
